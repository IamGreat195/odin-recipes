Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish made with a yeasted flatbread dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It's a versatile dish that can be customized to suit any taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  3. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  4. Transfer dough to a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  5. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese and pepperoni slices.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
